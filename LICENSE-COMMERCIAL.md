## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I evaluate the software before purchasing a commercial license?

**A:** Absolutely! The SITL (Software-In-The-Loop) simulation demo is free to use for evaluation purposes. Test all features, run extensive simulations, and make sure it meets your needs before committing to a commercial license.

### Q: What if I'm a small startup or indie developer?

**A:** We understand the challenges of small businesses. Contact us to discuss your situation. We offer flexible terms and can work with startups through graduated licensing that scales with your growth.

### Q: Can I modify the software under a commercial license?

**A:** Yes! Commercial licenses typically grant you the right to modify the software for your own use. The specific modification rights depend on the license tier you choose.

### Q: What if I need custom features or integrations?

**A:** We offer custom development services as part of Enterprise licenses or as separate professional services. We can develop custom features, integrations, or modifications specific to your requirements.

### Q: Do you offer free trials for commercial use?

**A:** The SITL demo serves as your evaluation period. For actual commercial deployment, we can discuss pilot project arrangements or phased licensing if appropriate for your use case.

### Q: Can I use this for internal corporate R&D?

**A:** Internal corporate use, even for R&D, is typically considered commercial use and requires a commercial license. However, if you're a university researcher working within a corporate research lab, contact us to discuss your specific situation.

### Q: What's included in "support"?

**A:** Support level varies by license tier:
- **Basic:** Email support for licensing and basic usage questions
- **Priority:** Faster response times, technical implementation help
- **Enterprise:** Dedicated support contact, custom development assistance

### Q: Can I get a perpetual license instead of annual?

**A:** Yes, we can discuss perpetual licensing options, particularly for Enterprise licenses. Pricing and terms will be adjusted accordingly.

### Q: Do you offer educational discounts for commercial training programs?

**A:** If you're running a commercial training program that teaches drone technology, contact us. We evaluate these on a case-by-case basis as they support education while being commercial in nature.

### Q: What happens if I use the software commercially without a license?

**A:** Using the software commercially without a proper license is a violation of the CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license and copyright law. We prefer to work with users proactively. If you've started using it commercially, contact us immediately to rectify the situation.

### Q: Can I white-label or rebrand the software?

**A:** White-labeling and rebranding are possible under Enterprise licensing agreements. We can discuss the specific terms for your use case.

### Q: Do commercial licenses include the source code?

**A:** Yes, commercial licenses include access to the source code, allowing you to understand, modify, and deploy the software according to your license terms.
