## SAFETY WARNING

**Drones Are Inherently Dangerous**

Operating drones, especially in autonomous or swarm mode, carries significant safety risks. This software controls real aircraft that can cause serious harm.

**Before Any Real-World Use:**
- Extensively test in simulation (SITL) environment
- Conduct controlled tests in safe, isolated areas
- Have experienced pilots supervise initial flights
- Implement multiple layers of failsafe protection
- Follow manufacturer safety guidelines
- Comply with all applicable regulations

**Never:**
- Operate near people, buildings, or airports without proper authorization
- Fly beyond visual line of sight without proper certification
- Disable safety features or failsafes
- Operate under influence of drugs or alcohol
- Exceed your skill and experience level
- Ignore weather conditions or equipment warnings
