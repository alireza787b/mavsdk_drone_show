## Eligibility Requirements

### Requirement 1: Employee/Contractor Count

**Your company must have:**
- **Fewer than 100** total individuals working as:
  - Employees (full-time, part-time)
  - Independent contractors
  - Consultants
  - All people doing work for your company

**Counting:** Include everyone working for your company and related entities under common control.

### Requirement 2: Revenue Threshold

**Your company must have:**
- **Less than $1,000,000 USD** total revenue in the prior tax year

**Inflation Adjustment:**
- Base year: 2019
- Adjust using US Bureau of Labor Statistics CPI-U (Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers)
- Not seasonally adjusted, 1982-1984=100 reference base

**2025 Adjusted Threshold:** Approximately $1,150,000 USD (check CPI for exact figure)

### Requirement 3: Drone Count (MAVSDK Specific)

**Your drone operations must:**
- **Use fewer than 10 drones** in any single operation

**What counts as "operation":**
- Any single flight mission
- Any drone show or event
- Any coordinated swarm activity
- Maximum drones under simultaneous control
- Any single cohesive mission regardless of duration

**Example scenarios:**

‚úÖ **ALLOWED (< 10 drones):**
- Drone show with 8 drones
- Inspection with 5 drones
- Photography with 3 drones
- Multiple separate 6-drone shows (not simultaneous)

‚ùå **NOT ALLOWED (requires commercial license):**
- Drone show with 12 drones
- Swarm with 10 drones
- Two simultaneous 6-drone operations (12 total)
- Any operation with 10+ drones

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I have 8 employees and 5 drones. Do I qualify?**
A: Yes! You meet all thresholds. Free to use.

**Q: I have 50 employees but use 12 drones. Do I qualify?**
A: No. You exceed the 10-drone threshold. Need commercial license.

**Q: What if I run two separate 6-drone shows on the same day?**
A: If they're completely separate operations at different times/locations, that's two < 10-drone operations. OK.
If they're coordinated or simultaneous, that's 12 drones total. Need commercial license.

**Q: Can I test with 15 drones in SITL simulation?**
A: Yes! SITL simulation for testing doesn't count as "operation." Test with as many as you want.

**Q: My revenue was $900K last year but will exceed $1M this year.**
A: You qualify now based on prior year. When you exceed $1M, contact us for commercial license going forward.

**Q: I'm a startup with $2M in funding but $0 revenue. Do I qualify?**
A: Based strictly on revenue: yes. But large, well-funded startups should contact us for commercial licensing.

**Q: What if I'm unsure?**
